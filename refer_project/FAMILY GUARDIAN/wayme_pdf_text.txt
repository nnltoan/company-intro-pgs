WayMe is Your outdoor companion for reaching your destination safely. WayMe drives to

Role, Objective and Challenge 
Become our trusted freelance engineer and pioneer to drive our hiking safety and live-
tracking app combines advanced geofencing, off-route alerts, and real-time location sharing 
to protect hikers, runners, and outdoor enthusiasts. With a database of curated trails, group 
tracking features, and safety-first innovations like off route alerts and progress monitoring, 
WayMe empowers individuals and communities to explore confidently the outdoor.  
Key tasks 
Research and develop out of our core technology applicable solutions for the specific 
requirements and integration in outdoor usage to provide (online and offline) precise 
location, safety tracking, navigation and orientation guidance and performance data for the 
users of our platform. Work together with our remote development team.
